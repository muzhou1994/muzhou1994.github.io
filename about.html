In 2016, I earned a bachelor's degree from Xiamen University.
Following my graduation, I began my career as a primary Java engineer at Meili Inc, a Hangzhou-based e-commerce fashion enterprise that is renowned for its consumer platform, Mogujie.

Now my plan is to create some English articles which will serve two purposes: for my career advancement and for improving my proficiency in English.
